Safety Measures

  • Swimming & Shoreline Walking: While the beach is generally safe, avoid swimming during rough tides and always stay within marked zones. Be cautious of jellyfish or slippery rocks near the shore.
  • Lighthouse & Jetty Visits: Exercise care when walking around the old jetty or climbing the lighthouse area. Avoid entering restricted or eroded sections.
  • Photography & Sun Gazing: For sunrise or sunset photography, carry proper lighting gear and avoid rocky or unstable spots. Stay alert after dark and keep valuables safe.
  • Seafood Tasting: Opt for fresh, hygienically prepared food at well-reviewed stalls to avoid foodborne illness. Carry drinking water to stay hydrated.
  • Bird Watching: Maintain a respectful distance from wildlife and avoid disturbing habitats. Use binoculars for observation instead of approaching birds too closely.
  • General: Keep emergency contact numbers handy. Avoid isolated areas after dark, and respect local customs and environmental rules.
