Safety Measures

  • Beach Games: Play only in designated open areas. Avoid slippery rocks and be cautious during peak sun hours to prevent heatstroke.
  • Yoga & Meditation: Use mats on even ground. Stay hydrated and avoid sessions during extreme heat. Choose shaded areas during midday hours.
  • Trekking: Wear proper footwear and avoid trekking during rain or after sunset. Carry a flashlight and follow marked trails.
  • General: Respect the natural environment. Avoid littering and be cautious around monkeys or stray animals. Use sunscreen and drink plenty of water.
