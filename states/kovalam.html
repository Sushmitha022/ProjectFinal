Safety Measures

  • Surfing: Always wear a leash and use a board suited for your skill level. Check for flags or signs on wave conditions.
  • Parasailing: Ensure all equipment is checked by certified operators. Follow all instructions provided before take-off.
  • Catamaran Rides: Wear a life jacket at all times. Avoid standing or sudden movements during the ride.
  • Swimming: Swim only in designated zones under lifeguard supervision. Avoid strong currents or venturing too deep.
  • General: Stay hydrated, use sunscreen, and avoid venturing into the sea during rough weather or after sunset.
