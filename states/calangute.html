Safety Measures

  • Parasailing & Jet-Skiing: Always use certified operators and wear life jackets. Ensure proper harnessing for parasailing and avoid these activities during high winds or rough seas.
  • Water Surfing & Banana Boat Rides: Follow safety instructions carefully and check for proper equipment maintenance. Do not attempt these activities under the influence of alcohol.
  • Shopping at Calangute Market: Keep an eye on your belongings in crowded areas. Bargain respectfully and be aware of pickpockets during peak hours.
  • Beach Photography & Sunset Watching: Be mindful of slippery rocks and uneven terrain when positioning for photos. Keep a safe distance from the waterline, especially during high tide. Watch your belongings, and avoid isolated areas after dark.
  • Beach Volleyball & Sunbathing: Stay hydrated and apply reef-safe sunscreen. Avoid prolonged exposure during peak sun hours (12 PM to 3 PM), and check the sand temperature before barefoot activities.
  • General: Always follow lifeguard signals and beach safety flags. Carry minimal valuables and avoid going into the water after dark or during high tide alerts.
